What is Pair Programming with AI?

Pair Programming with AI is a structured methodology for working with artificial intelligence as a collaborative programming partner. Rather than treating AI as a shortcut tool that "just generates code," this approach teaches you how to orchestrate an effective working relationship with AI assistants like Cursor, ChatGPT, and Copilot.

Through real examples, prompt-building strategies, and an orchestration model, you'll learn how to think, plan, and create more effectively. The goal isn't to let AI do all the work—it's to amplify your abilities as a developer by treating AI as an intelligent teammate.

What You'll Learn

  • The orchestration model for effective AI collaboration
  • How to build and refine prompts iteratively
  • Real-world coding scenarios and video demonstrations
  • Best practices for treating AI as a programming teammate
  • Communication patterns that get better results from AI
  • When to lead, when to follow, and when to iterate
